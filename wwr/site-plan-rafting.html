Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information. This website will provide the different rivers available for rafting along with prices and difficulty level of each. There will also be an explanation of what White River Rafting entails, what supplies will be needed and what supplies will be provided.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target customers? are anyone who is interested in white water rafting both beginners and experienced rafters. What do they want? They will want information explaining white water rafting and what services are provided. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The questions that will be answered are how much will it cost, what rivers are available for rafting, and if white water rafting is right for them.
