Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information to anyone interested in White Water Rafting. The information provided will include what rivers we offer, length of trips, and experience level needed for each trip. We will also provide information on the diferent river rafting guides and their qualifications.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target customers are anyone who is interested in White Water Rafting. What questions will be answered by them accessing the website? This website will answer what trips are available and what they need to do to prepare if this is their first time. How will my audience usually access my website? I imagine that this website will be mostly accessed using mobile devices as people will want to access this information on the go or on the way to the river.
